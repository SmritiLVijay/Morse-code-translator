The Morse Coder

Encoded Text:


  • Instructions

  • Enter the message in the text box.
  • The Morse code is case-insensitive but traditionally capital letters are used.
  • Click on the "use slash" button to have words separated by '/' and "use space" button to have words separated by spaces.

  • International Morse Code

  • 1. The length of a dot is one unit.
  • 2. A dash is three units.
  • 3. The space between parts of the same letter is one unit.
  • 4. The space between letters is three units.
  • 5. The space between words is seven units.
